I was born in Littleton, Colorado, but raised in Sacramento, California. Shortly out of high school I performed numerous jobs ranging from the restaurant industry to the automobile industry. I also did construction and worked on petitioning for state bills, among many other various jobs.

My jobs have always been diverse, as have my travels been. I've traveled around the US quite a bit; at times just simply deciding to move and live somewhere else for the pure experience of it. My thirst for the awe of the moment soon found a place in my work life as well.

Out of sheer will I took it upon myself to craft my skills as a writer. I had always been fascinated with writing of all sorts so I decided to experience it for myself. After a couple years of practicing writing, with no degree mind you, I landed a couple jobs with some monthly literature magazines, a night life magazine, and even a local newspaper.

Writing for the local newspaper of my hometown is what led me into the Army; after being sent to cover a story at an Army base in Missouri. Just as I had always been interested in writing I was also interested in reconnasaince of sorts. Being a detective, investigative reporter, or Navy Seal were thing of my childhood dreams. Shortly after hearing about such a job in the US Army I took the plung and became a Cavalry Scout in the 10th Mountain Division.

I served three years in active duty and pulled one tour in Afghanistan. After an honorable discharge my curiosity had peaked once again. Growing up during the computer/internet boom of the 90's had engrained in me a deep fascination with computers and everything related. Just as I had done before with every major decision in my life; I saw something I wanted to do and set out to do it. Now I am currently enrolled at Weber State University as a Computer Science major and I'm loving every minute of it.
